How to Play the Memory Game

Objective

The objective of the memory game is to match all pairs of cards with as few moves as possible and as quickly as possible.

Setup

Face-down cards are arranged in a grid to play the game. There are matching pairs of each card somewhere on the board..

Rules

  1. To see the symbol on a card and turn it over, click on it.
  2. To reveal the symbol on a second card, click on it to turn it over.
  3. The cards stay face-up if they match.
    4. `
  4. >After a brief pause, the cards are flipped back face-down if the two do not match.
    5. `
  5. find every pair of matching cards, keep flipping cards.
    6. ` When every pair is located and every card is face-up, the game is over.

Controls

  • Click here. To find out a card's symbol, flip it over.
  • Put the game on hold: To put the game on hold, click the "Pause Game" button. To continue, click once more.
  • Restart the game: The "Reset Game" button allows you to start the game over from scratch.

Scoring

The quantity of moves you make and the amount of time it takes to finish the game determine your score. To get the highest score, try to match every pair in the lowest amount of time and with the fewest movements.

Tips

  • Make an effort to recall where the cards that you have already turned over are.
    • To prevent misunderstanding, concentrate on a single pair at a time.
  • To strengthen your memory, take your time and focus on the game.
