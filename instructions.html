How to Play the Memory Game

Objective

The objective of the memory game is to match all pairs of cards with as few moves as possible and as quickly as possible.

Setup

The game consists of a grid of face-down cards. Each card has a matching pair hidden somewhere on the board.

Rules

  1. Click on a card to flip it over and reveal its symbol.
  2. Click on a second card to flip it over and reveal its symbol.
  3. If the two cards match, they remain face-up.
  4. If the two cards do not match, they are flipped back face-down after a short delay.
  5. Continue flipping cards to find all the matching pairs.
  6. The game ends when all pairs are found and all cards are face-up.

Controls

  • Click: Flip a card over to reveal its symbol.
  • Pause Game: Click the "Pause Game" button to pause the game. Click again to resume.
  • Reset Game: Click the "Reset Game" button to restart the game from the beginning.

Scoring

Your score is based on the number of moves and the time taken to complete the game. Try to match all pairs with the fewest moves and in the shortest time possible to achieve the best score.

Tips

  • Try to remember the positions of the cards you have already flipped over.
  • Focus on one pair at a time to avoid confusion.
  • Take your time and concentrate on the game to improve your memory skills.
